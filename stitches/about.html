Company Profile

Stitches & Wear Industries is a garment manufacturer, wholesaler, and retailer specializing in t-shirts, factory uniforms, office wear, school uniforms, and sports uniforms. They also offer services for corporate giveaways, including embroidered towels, bags, and mugs.

The company was established in 1997 with the main objective of providing high-quality service and competitive prices to all institutions and end-users. They have over two decades of experience, having been in business for 23 years at the time the profile was written.

Vision

Stitches and Wear Industries aims to be the preferred company for corporate branding, uniforms, and promotional giveaways. The company strives to become popular with its unique and high-quality products in the industry.

Mission

The mission of Stitches and Wear Industries is to serve various institutions and end-users with the highest standards of quality, service, and delivery. The company is committed to providing innovative products and developing the skills of its people to produce better services for its clients.
